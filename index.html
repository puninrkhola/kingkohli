Introduction

Virat Kohli is widely regarded as one of the greatest cricketers of the modern era. His aggressive playing style, unmatched consistency, and fierce determination have made him a fan favorite around the world.

Career Highlights

Since making his debut in 2008, Kohli has achieved numerous milestones. He was part of the 2011 World Cup-winning squad, and he holds records for being the fastest to score 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 ODI runs. His ability to chase down targets under pressure has earned him the nickname "Chase Master."

Leadership and Impact

As captain, Kohli led India to significant Test victories overseas and built a fitness-driven, aggressive cricket culture. Under his leadership, the team developed a winning mindset, especially in Test cricket, where India became a dominant force.
